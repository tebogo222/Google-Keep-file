## ğŸ“– How to Use

### Creating a Note

1. On the main page, click the **"Take a note..."** box
2. Enter your note content
3. Optionally add a **title** at the top
4. Click save or press Enter

### Editing a Note

- Click on any existing note to open it
- Modify the title or content as needed
- Changes are saved automatically

### Deleting Notes

- Click the **delete icon** at the bottom of the note
- The note will be permanently removed

### Archiving Notes

- Click the **archive icon** at the bottom of the note
- Keep your workspace clean while preserving important notes
